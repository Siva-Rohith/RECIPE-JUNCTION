Masala Dosa

  • INGREDIENTS

  • For Dosa Batter:
  • Rice: 2 cups (preferably parboiled)
  • Urad dal: 1 cup (split black gram)
  • Fenugreek seeds: 1 teaspoon
  • Salt: to taste
  • Water: as needed for soaking and grinding
  • For Masala Filling:
  • Potatoes: 3 medium, boiled and mashed
  • Onion: 1 medium, finely chopped
  • Green chilies: 2, finely chopped
  • Ginger: 1-inch piece, grated
  • Curry leaves: a few, chopped
  • Turmeric powder: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Salt: to taste
  • Oil or ghee: for cooking

  • Preparation Process

  1. Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds in water for 6-8 hours or overnight.
  2. Drain the soaked ingredients and grind them together to a smooth batter, adding water as needed. The consistency should be similar to pancake batter.
  3. Transfer the batter to a bowl, add salt, and let it ferment in a warm place for 8-12 hours, or until it rises and becomes slightly bubbly.
  4. For the filling, heat oil in a pan. Add curry leaves, onion, green chilies, and ginger. Saut√© until the onion turns translucent.
  5. Add the mashed potatoes, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for a few minutes. Set aside.
  6. To make dosas, heat a non-stick pan or griddle. Pour a ladleful of the dosa batter and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin layer.
  7. Drizzle some oil or ghee around the edges and cook until the dosa is golden brown and crispy.
  8. Place a portion of the potato filling in the center of the dosa and fold it over. Cook for another minute.
  9. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
