Pulao

  • INGREDIENTS

  • Rice: 1.5 cups (Basmati or long-grain)
  • Water: 3 cups
  • Vegetables: 1.5 cups (carrots, peas, beans, etc., chopped)
  • Onion: 1 medium, thinly sliced
  • Ginger-garlic paste: 1 tablespoon
  • Whole spices:
    • Cumin seeds: 1 teaspoon
    • Bay leaf: 1
    • Cloves: 4-5
    • Cardamom: 2-3
    • Cinnamon stick: 1-inch piece
  • Salt: to taste
  • Oil or ghee: 2 tablespoons
  • Coriander leaves: for garnish

  • Preparation Process

  1. Rinse the rice under running water until the water runs clear. Soak it in water for 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. In a large pot, heat oil or ghee. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add the whole spices (bay leaf, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon) and sauté for a minute until fragrant.
  4. Add the onion and sauté until golden brown.
  5. Stir in the ginger-garlic paste and cook for another minute.
  6. Add the chopped vegetables and sauté for a few minutes until they are slightly tender.
  7. Add the drained rice and gently mix to coat the rice with the spices.
  8. Add water and salt. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and cover the pot.
  9. Cook for about 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is fully cooked and water is absorbed.
  10. Once done, fluff the pulao with a fork and garnish with coriander leaves. Serve hot with raita or salad.
