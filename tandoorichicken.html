Tandoori Chicken

  • INGREDIENTS

  • Chicken: 1 kg, cut into pieces (preferably with bone)
  • Yogurt: 1 cup
  • Ginger-garlic paste: 1 tablespoon
  • Red chili powder: 1 tablespoon (adjust to taste)
  • Turmeric powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Cumin powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Coriander powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Garam masala: 1 teaspoon
  • Lemon juice: 2 tablespoons
  • Salt: to taste
  • Vegetable oil: 2 tablespoons (for brushing)
  • Fresh cilantro: for garnish
  • Lemon wedges: for serving

  • Preparation Process

  1. Marinate the Chicken: In a large bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, garam masala, lemon juice, and salt to create a marinade. Add the chicken pieces and ensure they are well coated. Cover and marinate in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours or overnight for best results.
  2. Preheat the Grill or Oven: Preheat your grill or oven to a high temperature (around 200째C/400째F).
  3. Prepare the Chicken for Cooking: Remove the chicken from the marinade and shake off excess. Thread the marinated chicken onto skewers if grilling. If using an oven, place the chicken pieces on a baking tray lined with aluminum foil.
  4. Cook the Chicken: Grill or bake the chicken for about 25-30 minutes, turning occasionally and brushing with vegetable oil, until the chicken is cooked through and has a nice char. Use a meat thermometer to ensure the internal temperature reaches 75째C (165째F).
  5. Garnish and Serve: Once cooked, remove the chicken from the skewers or tray. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with lemon wedges and naan or rice.
