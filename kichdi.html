Kichdi

  • INGREDIENTS

  • Rice: 1 cup
  • Moong dal (yellow split gram): 1/2 cup
  • Water: 4 cups
  • Turmeric powder: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Salt: to taste
  • Ghee or oil: 2 tablespoons
  • Cumin seeds: 1 teaspoon
  • Ginger: 1-inch piece, grated
  • Vegetables: 1 cup (optional, chopped carrots, peas, etc.)

  • Preparation Process

  1. Rinse the rice and moong dal together under running water until the water runs clear.
  2. In a pressure cooker, heat ghee or oil. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add the grated ginger and sauté for a minute until fragrant.
  4. Add the rinsed rice and moong dal to the cooker, and sauté for a couple of minutes.
  5. Add turmeric powder, salt, and water. Stir well and bring to a boil.
  6. If using vegetables, add them at this stage.
  7. Close the lid of the pressure cooker and cook for about 2-3 whistles, or until the rice and dal are soft.
  8. Once done, release the pressure and fluff the khichdi with a fork. Serve hot with yogurt or pickle.
