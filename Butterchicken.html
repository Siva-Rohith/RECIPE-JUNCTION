Butter Chicken

  • INGREDIENTS

    • Chicken: 500 grams, boneless, cut into cubes
    • Yogurt: 1/2 cup
    • Lemon juice: 1 tablespoon
    • Ginger-garlic paste: 1 tablespoon
    • Red chili powder: 1 teaspoon
    • Garam masala: 1 teaspoon
    • Salt: to taste
    • Butter: 3 tablespoons
    • Onions: 2 medium, finely chopped
    • Tomato puree: 1 cup
    • Fresh cream: 1/2 cup
    • Cashews: 10-12, soaked and ground to a paste
    • Kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves): 1 teaspoon
    • Honey: 1 tablespoon (or sugar to taste)
    • Fresh cilantro: for garnish

  • Preparation Process

  1. Marinate the Chicken: Mix chicken with yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Marinate for at least 1 hour or overnight in the refrigerator.
  2. Cook the Chicken: Heat 1 tablespoon of butter in a pan and cook the marinated chicken until it is browned on all sides. Remove and set aside.
  3. Prepare the Gravy: In the same pan, add the remaining butter and saut√© chopped onions until golden. Add tomato puree, cashew paste, and cook until the oil starts to separate.
  4. Simmer the Sauce: Add garam masala, kasuri methi, honey, and salt to taste. Let it simmer for 5-7 minutes until flavors are well combined.
  5. Add Chicken and Cream: Add the cooked chicken and cream to the gravy. Simmer for another 5-10 minutes until the chicken is tender and coated in the creamy sauce.
  6. Garnish and Serve: Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.
