Chaat

  • INGREDIENTS

  • Onion: 1 medium, finely chopped
  • Tomato: 1 medium, finely chopped
  • Cucumber: 1 medium, finely chopped
  • Boiled potatoes: 2 medium, diced
  • Chickpeas: 1 cup, boiled
  • Green chilies: 1-2, finely chopped
  • Coriander leaves: a handful, chopped
  • Chaat masala: 1-2 teaspoons
  • Tamarind chutney: 2 tablespoons
  • Yogurt: 1/2 cup, whisked
  • Sev: for garnish
  • Salt: to taste
  • Lemon juice: 1 tablespoon (optional)

  • Preparation Process

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the boiled potatoes, chickpeas, onion, tomato, cucumber, and green chilies.
  2. Add chaat masala, salt, and lemon juice (if using). Mix well to combine all the ingredients.
  3. Drizzle tamarind chutney and whisked yogurt over the mixture. Gently fold to incorporate without mashing the potatoes.
  4. Garnish with coriander leaves and top with sev for added crunch.
  5. Serve immediately in individual bowls and enjoy!
